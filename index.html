F.E.C. 2020 CANDIDATE FUNDING SEARCH

Search a Senate or House candidate to find their committees, how much they've raised, and the top 20 occupations/amounts of the donors who have contributed to those committees. Case matters. Results appear below.

Background

This is an ongoing javascript learning project by Craig Varley searching the Federal Election Commission's API to find their active funding committees for the 2020 elections. It's active and as such has a few bugs, particularly around candidate names in retrieving the Wikipedia image for the funding profile. You can find it on Github and let me know if you have ideas or want to help out.
